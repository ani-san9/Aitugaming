The further away - the more victories our guys will be able to earn

We believe that hard work and constant training will lead our players to great heights in esports. Therefore, our task is to help the players with their training and create all the conditions for our esports players to bring more victories and titles to our university and club


How we will do it?


Conducting training games of our students with students of other universities.

Dialogue with the university, which will allow our players to freely participate in tournaments without worrying about attendance.

Moral support of the whole club, spectators at tournament broadcasts, personal support from the administrators of the club.
