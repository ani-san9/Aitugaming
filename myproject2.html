About AITU Gaming

The club was founded in 2019 by first-year students to diversify student life. At the moment, the club is the largest in the university and gathers 200-300 more people every year.

This club is a great opportunity to make new friends, have discussions about your favorite games and hobbies, play together with other students and hone your skills to become the best at the university! Many different events held by our club allow new students and club members to easily and quickly adapt to the new environment

Visit our university's website

Gaming Career

Our club and the university are doing their best to help the players achieve their goals, which is why there is such a friendly atmosphere in the club.

Already today, thanks to the activities of the club, our young talents participate in dozens of different tournaments, which allows them to find teams and new friends, as well as improve themselves to take prizes at these tournaments. Today, our teams in games such as CS GO, DOTA 2 and CLASH ROYALE have brought many important victories and awards to our university, for which we thank them very much.
